History

Markov chains are named after their creator, Andrey Markov. They are mathematical systems that "hop" from one state to another. In other words, it can be used to anticipate future events from a current state. Markov initially designed Markov chains in order to analyze the frequencies of vowels and consonants in the novel Eugene Onegin.
